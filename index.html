About Me

Aerospace Engineer

I'm Dinuka Hannadige, an aerospace engineering student at CU Boulder studying Aerospace Engineering Sciences with a minor in Engineering Management. I care about building things that work under real conditions, whether that's a competition aircraft, a heated ski wax tool, or a racing car aerodynamics package.

I'm a Propulsion Engineer on Colorado Design Build Fly and an Engineering Senator in the University of Colorado Student Government (CUSG) Legislative Council, where I represent the College of Engineering, work on legislation, and participate in the allocation of student fees across campus. I also work as an Engineering Peer Coach in the College of Engineering and have done work with BAE Systems in their Space and Mission Systems group.
